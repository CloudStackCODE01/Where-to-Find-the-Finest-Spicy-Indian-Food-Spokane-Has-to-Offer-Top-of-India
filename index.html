What Makes Our Spicy Indian Food Spokane’s Favorite?

Our menu features a variety of spicy Indian dishes that cater to different palates while keeping the bold flavors intact. Here are a few highlights:

  • Chicken Vindaloo: Known for its intense spiciness, this Goan specialty blends vinegar, garlic, and a potent mix of chili peppers. Our version balances heat with tangy undertones, making it a favorite among those who appreciate a fiery kick.
  • Lamb Rogan Josh: Slow-cooked lamb in a rich sauce infused with aromatic spices like cardamom and cinnamon. This dish offers a deep, warm spice profile that’s complex without being overwhelming.
  • Paneer Tikka Masala: For those who prefer vegetarian options, our paneer tikka is marinated in spices and grilled to perfection, then simmered in a creamy tomato sauce with just the right amount of heat.
  • Spicy Tandoori Chicken: Marinated with yogurt and a blend of chili powders and spices, this dish is cooked in a traditional tandoor oven, giving it a smoky and spicy flavor.

Each of these dishes highlights our dedication to maintaining the true essence of Indian spices while tailoring the heat to meet our customers’ preferences.
